Meet Chef Mariam
Hi, I'm Mariam — a chef fueled by passion and a love for all things delicious! I hold a Culinary Arts certification and have 2 years of professional kitchen experience, but my love for cooking began over 6 years ago. What started as a hobby quickly became a lifelong passion, inspiring me to explore new flavors, techniques, and cuisines.

Today, I'm the founder of Jinro's Kitchen, where I share easy, flavorful recipes, cooking tips, and food inspiration through my website and YouTube channel. My goal is simple: to make cooking approachable, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone.

Whether you're just beginning your culinary journey or you're already comfortable in the kitchen, I'm so happy you're here.

My Story
Although I'm currently based in Bangalore, my culinary journey began in Dubai, UAE, where I first discovered my love for cooking. Over the years, cooking became more than just a skill—it became my creative outlet, my comfort zone, and eventually my career. In 2024, I moved to Bangalore with a dream of launching my own cloud kitchen. That dream became a reality with Jinro's Kitchen, serving homestyle meals with a unique twist through food delivery platforms.

Like many entrepreneurs, I had my share of challenges. Due to health issues, I had to temporarily step away from the business and focus on recovery. While it was a difficult period, it also gave me time to reflect, heal, and reconnect with my passion for sharing food with others.

During that time, I revived my love for recipe blogging and creating cooking videos—something I had first started years ago under the name Veg Wizard. With a renewed sense of purpose, I brought everything together under the Jinro's Kitchen name.

I'm happy to share that after overcoming those challenges, Jinro's Kitchen is back and serving customers once again on both Zomato and Swiggy. Every step of this journey has strengthened my belief that passion, perseverance, and good food can bring people together.
The Story Behind The Name

Jinro's Kitchen is named in memory of someone very special—my beloved cat, Jinro. For 10 wonderful years, Jinro was more than just a pet. He was family, my constant companion, and a source of unconditional love and comfort. In March 2024, I lost him, and just a few months later, I found myself starting a new chapter in Bangalore with the launch of my cloud kitchen.

Naming my business Jinro's Kitchen felt like the perfect way to keep his memory close and honor the joy he brought into my life. Every recipe, every meal, and every milestone carries a small piece of that love forward. His spirit remains at the heart of everything I create, reminding me that food, like love, has a way of bringing comfort, connection, and happiness to the people around us.
